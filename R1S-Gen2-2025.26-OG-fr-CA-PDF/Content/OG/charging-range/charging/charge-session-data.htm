Check Charge Session Data

To view charging information, go to Energy > Charging > Graph on the center display or the mobile app.

While the vehicle is charging, you can view the estimated range and detailed charge session data on the vehicle displays or in the mobile app.

Charge session data includes the following:

  • End time or time left to charge

  • Energy added (mi, km, or kWh)

  • Charge rate (mi/hr, km/hr, or kW)

  • Charging cost, if applicable

Time to charge varies depending on the following:

  • Power voltage

  • Current

  • Air temperature outside the vehicle

  • Battery temperature and preconditioning
