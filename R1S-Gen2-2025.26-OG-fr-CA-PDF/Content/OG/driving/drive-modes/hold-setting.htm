Hold

Hold is on by default in all drive modes. You can turn off Hold in Rock Crawl mode (if equipped).

  • On: Hold helps to stop the vehicle and keeps it stationary even if you don't have the brake pedal pressed. Press the brake pedal as needed. (See Vehicle Hold.)

  • Off: Release the brake pedal to roll freely. Press the brake pedal to slow, stop, or keep the vehicle stationary.
