About Media Sources

You can choose from several media sources, including FM or HD Radio™, Apple Music, Audible, SiriusXM®, Spotify®, TIDAL®, and TuneIn®. You can also pair to a device with Bluetooth® technology and adjust the audio settings.

With Rivian Premium Audio, Apple Music and Audible offer select content in Spatial Audio with Dolby Atmos®.

Some audio sources require the Connect+ package or a personal Wi-Fi hotspot: Apple Music, Audible, Spotify, Tidal, TuneIn.

  1. Choose Media on the menu from the center display.

  2. Select a media source on the left.
