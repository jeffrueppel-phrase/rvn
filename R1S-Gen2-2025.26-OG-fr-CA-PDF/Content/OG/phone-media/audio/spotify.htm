Spotify

You must log in the first time you use the app.

  1. Choose Media on the menu from the center display.

  2. Choose Spotify.

  3. Open your account on your phone or other device.

    Your vehicle must be in P (Park) and have cellular or Wi-Fi connectivity.

  4. Scan the QR code to sign in.

Spotify Queue

View the queue with the Queue icon . To add tracks to the queue, open a playlist or album and choose Add to Queue next to a track. You can remove tracks from the queue.

Streaming Quality

  1. Choose Music streaming quality.

  2. Select from five streaming levels:

    • Automatic

    • Low

    • Normal

    • High

    • Very High
