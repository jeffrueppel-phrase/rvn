Google Cast

When you are parked, you can use Google Cast™ to view video content from your phone or other connected devices on the vehicle's center display and audio system.

You can connect up to eight devices at a time.

Set Up Google Cast

  1. Put the vehicle in P (Park).

  2. Go to Video > Google Cast and choose Set it up.

  3. Select Accept for the Terms & Conditions and Privacy notice. You can select Not now to exit without setting up Google Cast.

  4. Turn on the hotspot if it's off.

  5. Scan the QR code to connect to the hotspot.

Use Google Cast

  1. On your mobile phone, launch a Cast-enabled application.

  2. Select Google Cast .

  3. Select Rivian as the target.

  4. Tap the screen anywhere to open the video control bar. Use it to:

    1. Increase or decrease volume: Choose the – and + buttons or slide

    2. Increase or decrease brightness: Choose the – and + buttons or slide

  5. Other video control bar options include:

    1. Move the control bar to the bottom using the grabber button at the far left.

    2. Choose the expand button to view the top status bar and the bottom app tray.

    3. Select EXIT to leave Google Cast.

  6. You can also disconnect from Google Cast with your phone.
