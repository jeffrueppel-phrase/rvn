YouTube

When you are parked, you can view YouTube™ on the center display.

You can sign into your account in the YouTube app to access all of your personalized content and recommendations.

  1. Put the vehicle in P (Park).

  2. Go to Video and choose YouTube on the left.

  3. Select Start watching.

  4. Tap the screen anywhere to open the video control bar. Use it to:

    1. Increase or decrease volume: Choose the – and + buttons or slide

    2. Increase or decrease brightness: Choose the – and + buttons or slide

  5. Other video control bar options include:

    1. Move the control bar to the bottom using the grabber button at the far left.

    2. Choose the back button to see a previous screen or to exit.

    3. Choose the expand button to view the top status bar and the bottom app tray.

    4. Select EXIT to leave YouTube.

    6. You can also watch YouTube through Google Cast.
