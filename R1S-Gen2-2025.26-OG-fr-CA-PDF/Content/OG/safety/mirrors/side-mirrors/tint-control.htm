Tint Control

The driver side mirror automatically tints at night in response to ambient lighting sensors in the rearview mirror (for example, when bright headlights shine from a vehicle behind you). Go to Settings  > Vehicle > Mirrors on the center display to enable or disable this function.

  • The side mirror doesn't tint when the vehicle is in R (Reverse) or
    P (Park).

  • In some vehicles, the passenger side mirror also tints automatically.
