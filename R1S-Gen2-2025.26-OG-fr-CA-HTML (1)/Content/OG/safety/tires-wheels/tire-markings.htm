Tire Markings

The following image describes tire markings for a typical tire. The positions of the tire markings may vary by tire manufacturer.

Item

Description
1

Tire type. A "P" marking indicates a passenger tire. A "HL" marking indicates a high load tire.
2

Nominal width in millimeters, measured from the outer edges of the sidewall.
3

Nominal aspect ratio (%). This describes the height of the tire expressed as a percentage of the tire width.
4

Internal construction. The "R" indicates a radial ply tire.
5

Rim diameter in inches
6

Load index
7

Speed symbol
8

Mud and snow grading
9

Maximum load rating
10

Maximum permissible inflation pressure
11

US DOT tire identification number
