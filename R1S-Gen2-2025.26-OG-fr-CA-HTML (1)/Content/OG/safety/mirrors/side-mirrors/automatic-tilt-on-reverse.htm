Side Mirror Auto-Tilt in Reverse

Use side mirror auto-tilt to provide better side mirror views of surroundings and curbs when you parallel park the vehicle or back it into a spot. This feature is disabled by default. To enable it:

  1. Go to Settings > Vehicle > Access on the center display.

  2. Turn on Side mirror auto-tilting.

With the feature enabled, both the driver and passenger side mirrors tilt when you shift the vehicle into R (Reverse). After you park the vehicle and shift it out of reverse, the side mirrors return to their normal drive position.

Customize the Side Mirror Auto-Tilt Positions

  1. Go to Settings > Vehicle > Access on the center display.

  2. Under Side mirror auto-tilting, choose Customize. The mirrors move into the default factory tilt position, or if you customized in the past, into the last saved tilt position.

  3. Use the thumb controls on the steering wheel to adjust the mirrors.

  4. Choose Remember to save the new settings to your driver profile.

To return the side mirror auto-tilt settings to the factory default, choose Restore.

Cancel Side Mirror Auto-Tilt in Reverse

To cancel side mirror auto-tilt when the vehicle is in reverse, press the left thumb button on the right side of the steering wheel. If you need to cancel it when you are near a garage associated with the vehicle:

  1. Scroll the thumbwheel on the right side of the steering wheel downward until you see the side mirror indicator on the driver display.

  2. Press the left thumb button on the right side of the steering wheel to cancel.
