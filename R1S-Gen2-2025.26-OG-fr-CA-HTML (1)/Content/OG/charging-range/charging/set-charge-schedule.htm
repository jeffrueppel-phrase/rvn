Set a Charge Schedule

A charge schedule helps you benefit from off-peak electricity rates or to stagger charging times between multiple vehicles.

To set a charge schedule:

  1. Park at the location where you want to set a charge schedule.

  2. Go to Energy > Charging > Schedule on the center display or the mobile app.

  3. Choose Daily or Weekdays.

  4. Set the times to start and stop charging.

  5. Turn on the schedule.

  • Turn off the schedule to charge immediately at the saved location.

  • The charge schedule pauses when you are away from the saved location.

  • If you set the charge amperage when you set a charge schedule, the vehicle saves the setting for future scheduled charge sessions.
