NFC Lock and Unlock

Instead of using the key fob buttons to lock and unlock the vehicle, you can use the NFC (Near Field Communication) reader on the driver door handle to lock or unlock the driver door. Hold the back of the key fob against the reader mark on the handle and wait a few seconds to lock or unlock.

  • NFC lock and unlock only work on the driver door. Passenger doors do not have NFC readers.

  • NFC lock and unlock work even if the key fob battery is dead.

  • If the vehicle is asleep, touch the key fob to the driver door handle for up to 8 seconds.
