Source Selector

On the Media app, choose a media source on the left. If you would like even more sources to choose from, open the Source Selector to see and select from all available options. You can move sources into and out of your favorites list.

  1. Select the More icon to expand the left panel.

  2. The expanded view shows your current favorite sources under Favorites on the left and the remaining sources in the More column on the right.

  3. Select a source from either column.

  4. To move a source between columns, press and hold your selection and then drag it to the other column.

  5. The Favorites column can contain a maximum of five favorite sources. There is also one dynamic spot that you can open from the More section.

  6. Select X to close the expanded panel.
