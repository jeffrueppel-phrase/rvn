SiriusXM

Connect to SiriusXM® to listen to your favorite music channels, news, sports, and podcasts. You also have access to Pandora stations within the same app.

SiriusXM is delivered via cellular or Wi-Fi® connectivity (not satellite).

Setup

Here's how to get started.

  1. Be sure the vehicle is in P (Park).

  2. Choose Media on the menu from the center display.

  3. Choose SiriusXM.

  4. To start your SiriusXM free trial, select Share and Start Listening in the trial subscription panel on the center display.

Discover and Listen

The SiriusXM app appears on the center display in a grid format.

  • For You: Personalized stations based on your listening preferences.

  • Categories: Browse through categories including music, sports, news, and talk.

  • My Collection: Press and hold a station to add it to your favorites tab. Select Edit to manage your favorites.

  • Search: Select the microphone icon to search using voice or choose Type with Keyboard to enter it manually.

  • Media Controls: Use the media player to view the currently playing selection and access relevant playback controls. Press the left and right CH arrows at the bottom of the media player to select channels sequentially.

  • Settings: Manage your listener profiles, sports notifications, and other settings.

The Sports category provides not only event coverage but live scores as well.
