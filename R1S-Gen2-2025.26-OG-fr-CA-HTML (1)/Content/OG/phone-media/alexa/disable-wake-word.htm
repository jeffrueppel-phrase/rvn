Disable Wake Word

Alexa is the only supported voice service in Rivian vehicles. To disable the wake word, "Alexa," on the center display, do one of the following:

  • To open the Alexa panel, choose the Alexa icon at the top left of the status bar.

  • Go to Settings > Apps > Alexa.

Once you disable the wake word, you can speak a command by long-pressing the left thumb control on the steering wheel.

If you enable the wake word, the active Alexa icon appears at the top left of the status bar on the center display. If you disable the wake word, the mute icon appears.
