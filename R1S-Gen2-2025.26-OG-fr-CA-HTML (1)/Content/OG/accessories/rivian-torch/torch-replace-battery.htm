Replace the Battery

  • Power the torch with the Rivian-supplied battery only. To obtain a new rechargeable battery, contact Rivian.

  • Don't allow the bottom of the lithium-ion battery to touch a metal object. This could short-circuit the battery.

  • Don't dispose of the battery in a fire. To dispose of any used battery, follow the local regulations for the disposal of household hazardous waste.

  1. Unscrew the end cap (2).

  1. Remove the old battery and insert the new battery.

  2. Screw on the end cap.
