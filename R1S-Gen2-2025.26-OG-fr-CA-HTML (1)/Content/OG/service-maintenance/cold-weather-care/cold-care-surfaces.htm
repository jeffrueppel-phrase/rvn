Surfaces

If your vehicle is iced over, only use the climate control system such as heat and window defrost to thaw surfaces.

  • Don't pour hot water onto any glass surface to thaw the glass. This may cause the glass to crack.

  • Don't use an ice scraper on plastic parts on the vehicle's exterior, such as the air intake cover and the trim near the windshield wipers. This may scratch or damage the plastic and nearby fixtures.
