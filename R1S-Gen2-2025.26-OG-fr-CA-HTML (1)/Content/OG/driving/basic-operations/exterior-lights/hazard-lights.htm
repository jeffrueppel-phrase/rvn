Hazard Lights

Press the Hazard Lights button on the overhead console to turn on the hazard lights. Press the button again to turn the hazard lights off. See Emergency SOS.

Turn signals have priority over hazard lights. If the hazard lights are turned on and you push the lights stalk up or down to indicate a turn or lane change, the turn signals flash and the hazard lights turn off temporarily. After the turn signals stop, the hazard lights resume.
