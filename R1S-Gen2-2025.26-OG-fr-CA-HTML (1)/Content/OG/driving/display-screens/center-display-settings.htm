Display Settings

You can customize the time format, units of measure, and language settings on the center display.

Time Format

  1. From the center display, choose Settings > Vehicle > Language and Units.

  2. Select 12-hour or 24-hour.

Units of Measure

From the center display, choose Settings > Vehicle > Language and Units. You can select Imperial, Metric, or Custom to show a mix of both.

Language

From the center display, choose Settings > Vehicle > Language and Units. Select Language.

Alexa is only available in English.
