Driver Seat Position

If Roomy Entry and Exit doesn't work or you can't save your seat position to your Driver Profile, recalibrate to the original factory settings.

  1. If the second-row seat behind the driver seat is folded flat, raise it and slide it back.

    If you attempt to perform calibration without first raising and sliding back the second-row seat, the calibration may fail or damage the seats.

  2. Sit in the front passenger seat to avoid affecting the calibration.

  3. Go to Settings > Vehicle > Service on the center display.

  4. Under Driver Position Calibration, choose Driver Seat.

  5. Follow the instructions on the center display to complete the calibration.

    Readjust your Driver Profile seat and Roomy Entry and Exit settings as needed.
