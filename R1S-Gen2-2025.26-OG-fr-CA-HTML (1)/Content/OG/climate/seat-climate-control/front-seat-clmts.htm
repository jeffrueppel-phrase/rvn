Front Seat Climates

At the bottom of the center display, choose Seat Climate to change the climate settings of the driver or passenger seat.

Icon

Name

Description

Heat steering wheel

Heat the steering wheel for a limited time. (Available on the driver side only)

Ventilate seat

Ventilate the seat. Choose Ventilate or Seat Climate to cycle through levels of air ventilation, including off.

A ventilated seat recirculates the in‑cabin air.

Heat seat

Heat the seat. Choose Heat or Seat Climate to cycle through levels of heat, including off.

  • Depending on the ambient temperature, the vehicle displays the Seat Climate icon with the Heat or Ventilate icon but doesn't automatically heat or ventilate the seat.

  • Climate Power and seat climates are separate controls. If you turn off Climate Power, it doesn't affect the seats.
