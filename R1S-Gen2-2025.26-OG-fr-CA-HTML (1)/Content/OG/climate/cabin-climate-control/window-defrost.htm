Window Defrost

The vehicle offers defrost options for the front windshield and rear window areas, as well as a defog option for the front.

To defrost the windows, choose Front Defog or Defrost or Rear Defrost at the bottom of the center display.

To defog or defrost before you enter the vehicle, use the Rivian mobile app.

Defog or Defrost the Windshield

Icon

Name

Description

Front defog or defrost

Choose Front Defog or Defrost to cycle through the following:

  • Defog: Clear the front windshield of fogging. A blue icon indicates defog.

  • Defrost: Turn on maximum defrost settings and clear the front windshield using heat. A red icon indicates defrost.

  • Off: Turn off maximum defrost settings. The climate settings return to the previous settings.

Rear defrost

Clear the rear window and side mirrors using heat. The bottom of the front windshield also heats to help deice windshield wipers.
